# Opening a Trade

## From the Chart

1. Click the **BUY** or **SELL** button in the topbar
2. Set the lot size, SL, and TP in the trade dialog
3. Confirm the trade

## From an Opportunity

When an AI analysis produces opportunities:
1. Open the **Analyse** panel
2. Find the opportunity card
3. Click **Execute** to open the trade with pre-filled parameters
4. Adjust if needed and confirm

## Parameters

| Parameter | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Direction** | BUY (LONG) or SELL (SHORT) |
| **Lots** | Position size (AI recommends based on your risk settings) |
| **SL** | Stop Loss price level |
| **TP1** | First take profit target |
| **TP2** | Second take profit target (optional) |
