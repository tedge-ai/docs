# Managing Positions

## Open Positions

Open positions appear in the **Trades** panel and as overlays on the chart.

Each position shows:
- Symbol, direction, lots
- Entry price
- Current P&L (real-time)
- SL and TP levels (editable inline)

## Modifying a Position

- Click on the SL or TP value to edit it inline
- Changes are applied immediately

## Closing a Position

- Click the **X** button on the position row
- The position closes at the current market price
- AI Coach review is generated automatically
