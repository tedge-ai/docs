# Profile & Settings

The Profile page is your account hub in tedge.ai. From here you manage your personal information, subscription, trading preferences, and integrations with external services like Telegram and EdgeBridge.


## Account Settings

Your tedge.ai account is managed through Clerk, which handles authentication and profile data. From the Profile page, you can:

**Update your name and email** — Click on your name or email to edit. Email changes require verification through a confirmation link.

**Change your password** — If you signed up with email and password, you can update your password from the security section. If you signed up with Google or GitHub, password management is handled by those providers.

**Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** — Add an extra layer of security to your account. Clerk supports authenticator apps (TOTP) for 2FA. Once enabled, you will need both your password and a code from your authenticator app to log in.

**Profile picture** — Upload or change your avatar, which appears in the platform's top bar and in any collaborative features.

## Trading Preferences

The trading preferences section lets you adjust the settings you chose during onboarding:

**Trading style** — Change your primary style (Scalping, Intraday, Swing, Position). This updates the default timeframe favorites in the quick-access bar.

**Risk profile** — Adjust your default risk percentage per trade (Conservative 0.5%, Moderate 1%, Aggressive 2%). This affects the lot sizing recommendations in AI analysis opportunities and the execution dialog.

**Favorite symbols** — Add or remove symbols from your quick-access bar. Changes take effect immediately on the charts page.

> **Tip:** You can also star/unstar timeframes and symbols directly from the charts page. The Profile page provides a centralized view of all preferences in one place.

## Subscription & Credits

The subscription section displays your current plan, billing cycle, and AI credit balance:

- **Current plan** — Shows your tier (Free, Basic, Advanced, or Premium) and the monthly price.
- **Credit balance** — Your remaining AI credits for the current billing cycle, with a usage breakdown by feature type.
- **Upgrade / Downgrade** — Change your subscription tier. Upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated credit top-up.
- **Credit packs** — Purchase additional credits without changing your plan, useful when you need a temporary boost.

## Integrations

The integrations section shows the status of your external service connections:

### Telegram

Link your Telegram account to receive notifications and use bot commands. The pairing process takes about 30 seconds. See [Telegram Integration](/docs/profile/telegram) for detailed instructions.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Linked** | Your Telegram username is displayed, notifications are active |
| **Not linked** | Click "Link Telegram" to start the pairing process |

### EdgeBridge

View the status of your MT5 Bridge connection. This section shows whether EdgeBridge is currently connected, the last connection time, and a link to the Bridge page for management.

## Demo Account

The demo account section provides controls for your virtual trading environment:

- **Current balance** — Your virtual trading balance.
- **Starting capital** — Configure the amount your demo account resets to (default $10,000).
- **Reset button** — Reset your virtual balance to the configured starting capital. Open positions are closed and the balance returns to the starting amount. Trade history is preserved.


## Data & Privacy

tedge.ai stores your trading data (analyses, trades, preferences) securely. From the Profile page, you can:

- **Export your data** — Download your trade history and analysis results.
- **Delete your account** — Permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action is irreversible.

> **Warning:** Deleting your account removes all data permanently, including trade history, analyses, AI Coach reviews, and journal notes. Export your data first if you want to keep a record.
