# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### EdgeBridge won't start
- Ensure MetaTrader 5 is installed and running
- Check that no other instance of EdgeBridge is already running
- Try running as Administrator

### Connection keeps dropping
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your firewall allows outbound WebSocket connections
- EdgeBridge reconnects automatically — brief drops are normal

### Symbols not loading
- Make sure your MT5 terminal is logged in to a broker account
- Some symbols may need to be enabled in MT5's Market Watch

### Trades not executing
- Verify your MT5 account has trading permissions (not read-only/investor)
- Check that the symbol is available for trading on your broker
- Ensure sufficient margin in your account
