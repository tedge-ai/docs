# Your First Analysis

## Run an Analysis

1. Navigate to the **Charts** page
2. Select a symbol (e.g., BTC/USD)
3. Open the **Analyse** panel (bottom tab on mobile, right sidebar on desktop)
4. Choose a strategy from the dropdown (default: SMC)
5. Click the **Play** button

## What Happens

The AI agent will:
1. Analyze the H4 chart for macro bias
2. Analyze the H1 chart for intermediate structure
3. Analyze the M15 chart for actionable zones
4. Configure indicators adapted to the symbol
5. Identify and draw zones on your chart
6. Propose trading opportunities with entry, SL, TP

## Understanding the Results

### Zones on the Chart
- **Green rectangles** — Demand zones (buy areas)
- **Red rectangles** — Supply zones (sell areas)
- **Blue lines** — Key levels (S/R, pivots)
- **Orange lines** — Liquidity pools (stop hunts)

### Opportunities
Each opportunity includes:
- **Direction** — LONG or SHORT
- **Entry zone** — Price range for entry
- **SL** — Stop loss level
- **TP1 / TP2** — Take profit targets
- **R:R** — Risk-to-reward ratio
- **Probability** — LOW, MODERATE, or HIGH

### Indicators
The AI automatically configures EMA/SMA indicators adapted to the symbol.
When you switch timeframes, indicators adjust automatically.
