# Getting Started

Welcome to tedge.ai, the AI-powered trading intelligence platform designed for serious traders who want an analytical edge. Whether you trade crypto, forex, indices, or commodities, tedge.ai combines professional charting, multi-timeframe AI analysis, and intelligent trade management into a single, streamlined workspace.


## What is tedge.ai?

tedge.ai is a web-based trading platform that pairs real-time charting with an AI agent capable of reading market structure, identifying high-probability zones, and proposing trade setups complete with entry, stop loss, and take profit levels. The AI does not trade for you — it analyzes, recommends, and coaches. You remain in full control of every decision.

The platform is built around six core pillars:

- **Professional charting** with 21 timeframes, 15 drawing tools, and configurable indicators — all rendered on a clean, dark interface designed for extended screen time.
- **AI market analysis** powered by six distinct strategies including Smart Money Concepts, Supply & Demand, and Breakout Retest. The agent examines multiple timeframes, draws zones directly on your chart, and streams its reasoning in real time.
- **Trade management** in both demo and live modes, with full position tracking, partial closes at TP1/TP2, and drag-to-modify SL/TP levels.
- **AI Coach** that reviews every closed trade, scoring your execution on a 1-to-10 scale and offering specific, actionable feedback.
- **Smart alerts** with price alerts, push notifications, Telegram integration, and an automated analysis scheduler.
- **EdgeBridge** for MetaTrader 5 users, connecting your broker's live data and trade execution to the web platform via a secure tunnel.

## Who is tedge.ai for?

The platform is designed for retail traders with some market experience who want to sharpen their analysis process. It works particularly well for traders who follow price action, Smart Money Concepts, or supply-and-demand frameworks. Whether you scalp the 5-minute chart or hold swing trades on the daily, the AI adapts its analysis to your preferred timeframes and strategies.

## Quick Start

Getting from signup to your first AI analysis takes under five minutes:

1. **Create your account** at [app.tedge.ai/sign-up](https://app.tedge.ai/sign-up) using email, Google, or GitHub.
2. **Complete onboarding** by selecting your trading style, risk preferences, and favorite symbols.
3. **Run your first analysis** — open a chart, choose a strategy, and let the AI agent work.
4. **Review the results** — explore the zones drawn on your chart, read the opportunity cards, and decide whether to execute.


## What You'll Need

- A modern web browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge all work well.
- An email address (or Google/GitHub account) to create your account.
- Optionally, MetaTrader 5 on a Windows machine if you want live broker data via EdgeBridge.

> **Tip:** tedge.ai works as a Progressive Web App (PWA). On mobile, add it to your home screen for a native app experience with push notifications.

## Platform Requirements

tedge.ai runs entirely in the browser with no desktop software required (except EdgeBridge for MT5 integration). The platform is optimized for the following environments:

| Environment | Recommended |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Desktop browsers** | Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+ |
| **Mobile browsers** | Safari on iOS 16.4+, Chrome on Android 10+ |
| **Screen resolution** | 1280x720 minimum, 1920x1080 recommended |
| **Internet connection** | Stable broadband for real-time data |
