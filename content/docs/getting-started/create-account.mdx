# Create an Account

## Sign Up

1. Go to [app.tedge.ai/sign-up](https://app.tedge.ai/sign-up)
2. Sign up with your email or Google account
3. Verify your email address
4. You're in!

## Free Tier

Every new account starts with the **Free** tier:
- 3 symbols available (BTC/USD, XAU/USD, EUR/USD)
- Demo mode only
- 10 AI credits offered to test features
- All timeframes and drawing tools

## Upgrade

To unlock all symbols, live trading, MT5 Bridge, and more AI credits, visit the [Plans & Pricing](/docs/getting-started/subscription) page.
