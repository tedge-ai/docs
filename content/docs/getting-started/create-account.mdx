# Create an Account

Setting up your tedge.ai account takes less than a minute. The platform uses Clerk for authentication, giving you multiple secure sign-up options and built-in two-factor authentication.

## Sign Up

Navigate to [app.tedge.ai/sign-up](https://app.tedge.ai/sign-up) to create your account. You have three options:

### Email and Password

Enter your email address and choose a password. Clerk will send a verification email — click the link to activate your account. Check your spam folder if the email does not arrive within a few minutes.

### Google

Click the "Continue with Google" button to sign in with your existing Google account. No additional password is required, and your email is verified automatically.

### GitHub

Click "Continue with GitHub" to use your GitHub credentials. This is particularly convenient if you already use GitHub for other developer tools.


## First Login

After verifying your account, you will be redirected to the platform. On your first visit, the onboarding wizard launches automatically to configure your trading environment. This step is required before you can access the charts and analysis tools.

> **Tip:** Bookmark [app.tedge.ai](https://app.tedge.ai) for quick access. On mobile, use "Add to Home Screen" in your browser to install the PWA version.

## What's Included on the Free Tier

Every new account starts on the **Free** tier with enough access to explore the platform and test the AI:

| Feature | Free Tier |
|---------|-----------|
| Available symbols | 3 (BTC/USD, XAU/USD, EUR/USD) |
| Trading mode | Demo only |
| AI credits | 10 (one-time welcome bonus) |
| Timeframes | All 21 |
| Drawing tools | All |
| Active alerts | 3 |
| MT5 Bridge | Not available |

The free tier is not time-limited. You can use it indefinitely with the three included symbols and demo trading.

## Upgrading Your Account

When you are ready for more symbols, live trading, or additional AI credits, visit the [Plans & Pricing](/docs/getting-started/subscription) page to compare tiers. Upgrades take effect immediately, and your AI credit balance is topped up as soon as the subscription activates.

## Account Security

Your account is protected by Clerk's enterprise-grade authentication. You can enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from your profile settings for an additional layer of security. tedge.ai never stores your trading account credentials — live trading connections are handled entirely through the EdgeBridge desktop application.

> **Warning:** Never share your tedge.ai login credentials or EdgeBridge pairing code with anyone. The pairing code grants access to your MT5 terminal through the platform.
