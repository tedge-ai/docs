# Onboarding

After creating your account, the onboarding wizard helps configure your trading environment.

## Trading Style

Choose your primary trading style:

| Style | Timeframes | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Scalping** | M1, M3, M5, M15 | Quick trades, small moves |
| **Intraday** | M5, M15, H1, H4 | Within the day |
| **Swing** | H1, H4, D1 | Multi-day positions |
| **Position** | D1, W1, MN | Long-term trades |

Your favorite timeframes will be automatically configured based on your choice.

## Risk Profile

Set your default risk per trade:
- **Conservative** — 0.5% per trade
- **Moderate** — 1% per trade
- **Aggressive** — 2% per trade

This determines lot sizing recommendations in AI analyses.

## Favorite Symbols

Select the instruments you trade most frequently. They'll appear in your quick access bar.
