# Plans & Pricing

tedge.ai offers four subscription tiers designed to scale with your trading needs. Every account starts on the Free tier, and you can upgrade at any time from your profile page. All paid plans include a monthly allocation of AI credits and unlock progressively more features.

## Understanding AI Credits

AI-powered features consume credits from your monthly allocation. The cost varies by feature type:

| Feature | Credit Cost | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **AI Analysis** | ~0.30 – 0.70 | Varies with analysis depth and number of timeframes |
| **AI Chat message** | ~0.03 | Each message in a conversation |
| **AI Coach insight** | ~0.08 | Generated automatically on trade closure |

Credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. Unused credits do not roll over. If you run out of credits mid-month, you can purchase additional credit packs from your profile page without changing your plan.

> **Tip:** A Basic plan with 15 monthly credits gives you roughly 20-40 analyses per month depending on their complexity, plus casual AI Chat usage. Most active traders find the Advanced plan's 40 credits sufficient.

## Free Tier Details

The Free tier is permanent and not time-limited. It gives you full access to the charting engine with all 21 timeframes and all drawing tools, but restricts you to three symbols (BTC/USD, XAU/USD, EUR/USD) and demo trading only. The 10 AI credits are a one-time welcome bonus — once spent, you will need to upgrade to continue using AI features.

## What Each Paid Tier Unlocks

### Basic (19€/month)

The Basic tier is the entry point for active traders. It unlocks all symbols, live trading through EdgeBridge, and the AI Chat assistant. You get 15 AI credits per month, which covers regular analysis usage for a focused watchlist. The AI Coach provides post-trade reviews for every closed trade.

### Advanced (49€/month)

The Advanced tier is designed for traders who rely heavily on AI analysis. With 40 monthly credits, you can analyze your full watchlist multiple times per week. The scheduler unlocks 10 slots for automated analyses, and the AI Coach adds dashboard-level synthesis that tracks your performance patterns over time. You also get up to 15 indicators per chart and 100 active alerts.

### Premium (149€/month)

The Premium tier removes all limits. AI credits are unlimited, scheduler slots are unlimited, and you get access to custom strategy prompts — write your own analysis rules and the AI adapts accordingly. Support is dedicated, and the AI Coach provides personalized coaching based on your full trading history.

## Managing Your Subscription

Visit the **Profile** page on the platform to view your current plan, credit balance, and billing cycle. From there you can upgrade, downgrade, or purchase additional credit packs. Plan changes take effect immediately — when upgrading, you receive the credit difference for the current cycle right away.

> **Warning:** Downgrading your plan may disable features that exceed your new tier's limits. For example, if you have 50 active alerts and downgrade from Advanced to Basic, alerts beyond the 20-alert limit will be paused (not deleted) until you upgrade again or manually reduce the count.
