## AI Credits

AI features (analysis, chat, coaching) consume credits:
- **Analysis** — ~0.30-0.70 credits depending on depth
- **Chat message** — ~0.03 credits
- **Coach insight** — ~0.08 credits

Credits are included in your plan and can be recharged anytime.
