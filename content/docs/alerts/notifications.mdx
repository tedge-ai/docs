# Push Notifications

tedge.ai supports browser push notifications and Telegram messages to keep you informed about alert triggers, trade closures, and completed analyses — even when you are not actively looking at the platform.

## Browser Push Notifications

### Setup

1. Look for the bell icon in the top bar when you first visit the platform.
2. Click the bell icon to enable push notifications.
3. Your browser will display a permission dialog — click **Allow**.
4. The bell icon changes to indicate notifications are active.

Once enabled, you receive desktop or mobile notifications for all alert triggers, trade events (SL/TP hit, manual close), and scheduled analysis completions.


### PWA Installation (Mobile)

For the best notification experience on mobile, install tedge.ai as a Progressive Web App:

**On iOS (Safari):**
1. Open [app.tedge.ai](https://app.tedge.ai) in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Name it "tedge.ai" and tap Add.
5. Open the app from your home screen and enable notifications when prompted.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open [app.tedge.ai](https://app.tedge.ai) in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**).
4. Confirm the installation.
5. Open the app and enable notifications when prompted.

The PWA version receives push notifications in the background, just like a native app. You do not need to keep the browser tab open.

> **Tip:** PWA notifications on iOS require iOS 16.4 or later. If you do not see the notification permission prompt, check that your iOS version is up to date.

## Telegram Notifications

Telegram provides a reliable secondary notification channel that works across all your devices. It also unlocks bot commands for checking positions and running analyses from within Telegram.

### Linking Your Account

1. Go to the **Profile** page on the platform.
2. Click **Link Telegram** in the integrations section.
3. A link is generated — click it to open the @tedgeaibot conversation in Telegram.
4. Send `/start` to the bot to complete the pairing.
5. The platform confirms the connection, and your Telegram username appears in the integrations section.


### What You Receive

Once linked, Telegram receives the same notifications as browser push:

- **Price alert triggers** — The symbol, price, and condition that was met.
- **Trade closures** — Entry/exit prices, P&L, and the AI Coach score.
- **Scheduled analysis results** — A summary of opportunities found, with a link to view the full analysis on the platform.

### Telegram Bot Commands

The tedge.ai bot also responds to commands, giving you quick access to information without opening the platform:

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/positions` | List your currently open positions with P&L |
| `/stats` | Display your trading statistics (win rate, total P&L, average score) |
| `/analyse BTCUSD` | Run an AI analysis on the specified symbol and receive the results in Telegram |

> **Tip:** The `/analyse` command consumes AI credits just like running an analysis on the platform. The results are a text summary — for the full chart experience with zones and replay, open the platform.

### Unlinking Telegram

To disconnect your Telegram account, go to the **Profile** page and click **Unlink Telegram** in the integrations section. You will stop receiving Telegram notifications immediately, and bot commands will no longer work.
