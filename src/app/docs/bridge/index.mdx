# MT5 Bridge

EdgeBridge connects your MetaTrader 5 terminal to the Tedge platform for live data and trade execution.

## What It Does

- **Live prices** — Real-time bid/ask from your broker
- **All symbols** — Access your broker's full symbol list
- **Trade execution** — Open/close/modify positions from the web platform
- **Position tracking** — Real-time P&L on your MT5 positions

## Requirements

- Windows PC with MetaTrader 5 installed
- An MT5 account (demo or live)
- Basic plan or higher
- Internet connection

## How It Works

EdgeBridge runs on your Windows machine and connects to Tedge via a secure WebSocket tunnel. No port forwarding or NAT configuration needed — the connection is outbound only.
