# Strategies

| Strategy | Category | Description |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Smart Money Concepts** | Structure | Order blocks, FVG, liquidity sweeps, BOS/CHoCH |

## Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

The default strategy. The AI identifies:
- **Order Blocks** — Last opposing candle before an impulsive move
- **Fair Value Gaps** — Imbalances between 3 candles
- **Break of Structure** — Swing high/low breakout confirming trend
- **Liquidity pools** — Accumulated stops beyond obvious levels

## Supply & Demand

Pure price action approach:
- **Rally-Base-Rally / Drop-Base-Drop** — Continuation zones
- **Rally-Base-Drop / Drop-Base-Rally** — Reversal zones
- Fresh (untested) zones are prioritized

## Breakout Retest

Waits for a clean break of a key level, then enters on the retest:
- Identifies the range and its boundaries
- Confirms the breakout with momentum
- Sets entry at the retested level

## Range Trading

Exploits range-bound markets:
- Identifies upper and lower boundaries
- Uses Bollinger Bands and RSI for confirmation
- Buys at support + oversold, sells at resistance + overbought

## Trend Pullback

Follows the trend by entering on retracements:
- Confirms trend with EMA alignment (20/50/200)
- Enters on pullbacks to dynamic support (EMA levels)
- Higher-timeframe trend must be clear

## Key Levels

Minimalist approach focused on the most important levels:
- Psychological levels (round numbers)
- Swing highs/lows tested multiple times
- Daily/weekly highs and lows
