# AI Coach

## Post-Trade Review

Every time you close a trade (SL, TP, or manual), Edge AI Coach automatically analyzes your trade and provides:

### Entry Quality
Was the entry well-timed? Was it at a significant level? Was there confluence with the analysis zones?

### Position Management
Was the SL/TP placement appropriate? Was the duration reasonable? Did you exit at the right time?

### Overall Assessment
Global synthesis with actionable advice to improve.

### Score (1-10)

| Score | Rating | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Poor | Bad entry, poor management, significant loss |
| 4-5 | Mediocre | Notable mistakes in execution |
| 6-7 | Good | Reasonable execution, room for improvement |
| 8-9 | Very Good | Precise entry, good management, plan respected |
| 10 | Excellent | Exemplary execution |

**Important**: A losing trade can score well if the execution was good. A winning trade can score poorly if it was based on luck.

## Viewing Reviews

- In the **Trades** panel, closed trades show the AI score in the last column
- Click on any closed trade row to expand the full AI Coach review
- The review appears with entry quality, position management, and overall analysis

## Dashboard Synthesis (Advanced+)

The Dashboard integrates AI Coach data into the weekly synthesis:
- Average score trends
- Recurring patterns and mistakes
- Improvement suggestions based on your history
