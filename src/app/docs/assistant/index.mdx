# AI Assistant

## Overview

Chat with Edge AI about any market, strategy, or trading question. The assistant has context about the current symbol and your recent analyses.

## How to Use

1. Open the **Assistant** tab (bottom tab on mobile, right sidebar on desktop)
2. Type your question
3. The AI responds with context-aware analysis

## Example Questions

- "What's the current trend on BTC?"
- "Should I take this long setup?"
- "Explain the order block at 67000"
- "What's the risk if I enter here?"
- "Compare the H1 and H4 bias"

## Context Awareness

The assistant knows:
- The current symbol and timeframe
- Your latest AI analysis (zones, opportunities)
- Your open positions and trade history
- Your trading profile (risk preferences, style)

## Cost

Each message consumes ~0.03 AI credits. Available on Basic+ plans.
