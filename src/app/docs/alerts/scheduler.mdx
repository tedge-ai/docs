# Scheduler

## Automated Analyses

Schedule AI analyses to run automatically on your favorite symbols.

## Setup

1. Open the **Scheduler** panel (available in Advanced+ plans)
2. Click **+ Add Schedule**
3. Select the symbol
4. Choose the frequency (e.g., every 4 hours, daily at market open)
5. Save

## How It Works

- The scheduler runs analyses in the background
- Results are saved and available in the analysis panel
- Push/Telegram notifications sent when new opportunities are found
- Each scheduled analysis consumes AI credits

## Slot Limits

| Plan | Scheduler Slots |
|------|----------------|
| Free | — |
| Basic | 2 |
| Advanced | 10 |
